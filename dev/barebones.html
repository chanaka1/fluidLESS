About fluidLESS

fluidLESS is currently capable of spliting up its framework into 3 sections within the body consisting of a header, content and footer. Within each of these (primarly focusing on the content) it is possible for one to place multiple dynamic sections. Some current sections. Selections currently available for use include:

  1. Full width
  2. Split
  3. Third
  4. Quarter
